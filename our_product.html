Our Product

The 2025 Robot "Vera"

This is our robot for the 2025 BEST Robotics competition. Designed and built entirely by students, this robot is engineered for maximum efficiency, precision, and durability to tackle this year's game challenges.

  • It has an appendage.
  • 4 wheels to maximize power, steering, and power efficiency.
  • A specialized, custom made counter weight to balance the robot so it doesn't collapse.
  • Specialized large scooper to pick up game pieces efficiently.
  • Singular servo moves a gear which moves two adjacent wheels.
